General Information



About Us

Vent is a website that is accessible and available to anyone. This is a safe space to use where anyone can share their feelings and get help from our trained volunteers 24/7. Volunteers will connect emotionally and personally with people to make them feel heard and allow them to aleve their anxiety. Our volunteers are screened and trained to handle a wide range of conversations, from trauma to identity to mental health, school/work and many more topics. This site also provides meditation and mindfulness tips to reduce anxiety and/ or stress.



Our Mission

We aim to create an approachable, accessible and affordable atmosphere for those who are having a hard time coping with stress and/or anxiety. We hope to foster a safe environment so that those who have been harassed and/or abused can reach out and receive the help that they deserve. Our overall mission is to ensure that people get the help they need, in the way that they want to receive it no matter what the cause of seeking assistance is.
